OUR STORY

Idea to develop online quiz platform begins amid COVID-19 Pandemic. When all the schools and colleges were forced to shutdown due to lockdown, We experienced an exponential rise of digital platform in order to provide online Education to students. Educational Instituion were asking student to give online examination which are mostly basedd on Objective Questions. It makes easy for the stuednts as well as faculty to give their exams as well as to evaluate the student's knowledge in particular subjects. We, Adheesh & Ujjawal, Currently Pursuing Bachelor of Engineering degree from CHANDIGARH UNIVERSITY in the field of Computer Science. We decided to create platform, called as Coders Vidya which is responsive as well as quality content in order to provide cutting edge-technology with great user experience. We hope it would be help for many students to practice or give online quizzes on this website. Thanks for your kind visit. Really appreciated!
